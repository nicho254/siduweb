What is Sidu International Limited?

Sidu International is a microfinance institution providing loans, savings, and financial solutions to individuals and businesses.

Benefits of Using Sidu

We offer quick loan approvals, financial security, low-interest rates, and flexible repayment options.

How Can I Use My Loan?

You can use loans for business growth, emergency needs, school fees, medical expenses, and investment opportunities.

How to Register

Simply fill out our online form, upload KYC documents, pay KES 500, and start applying for loans.

Chama Loans and Savings

Groups can save together and qualify for collective investment loans with attractive interest rates.

Reasons for Saving

Save to secure your financial future, prepare for emergencies, and grow your wealth through interest-earning savings plans.

Why Choose Sidu?

We prioritize financial empowerment, security, and customer satisfaction with reliable services.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How long does loan approval take?
Loan approvals take between 24-48 hours once all KYC documents are verified.

2. What are the interest rates?
Our interest rates vary based on the loan type and repayment period. Contact us for specific rates.

3. Can I repay my loan early?
Yes, early repayment is allowed without penalties.

4. What happens if I miss a repayment?
We provide a grace period, but penalties apply for late payments.

5. Can I apply for multiple loans?
Yes, depending on your repayment history and credit score.
