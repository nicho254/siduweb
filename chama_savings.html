Types of Chamas

1. Investment Chama: This Chama focuses on growing wealth through business ventures, real estate, or stock investments.

2. Welfare Chama: Designed to support members in times of need, such as medical emergencies or family issues.

3. Rotational Savings (Merry-Go-Round): Members contribute money periodically, and one member takes the whole amount each round.

4. Business Chama: Members pool funds to start a collective business, sharing profits and reinvesting.

5. Women/Youth Empowerment Chama: Aimed at supporting women or youth in business, education, and personal development.
